About our company

Welcome to Wasim Consulting

Is your small business running as efficiently and as profitably as you had planned? Small business owners know that sometimes you have to “wear many hats” as an owner and operator of a small business. If you want to be able to focus on the growth and profitability of your business so you can scale up, it may be time to consider outsourcing your accounting needs.

We would like to welcome you to Wasim Consulting Financial Services, and say thank you for visiting our website. We encourage you to contact us with any questions you may have about our services, and be sure to check out the “Services” section to find out more about the services we can offer to your small business.

Our website serves as just one of the many ways we can leverage technology to help our clients get the most out of the bookkeeping and tax services we offer. Whatever needs your small business may have, we can help. Look to us for bookkeeping services, business or personal tax return preparation and filing, IRS and state audit representation, payroll processing and reporting, and small business ERP system setup & implementation.
